\section{Question No-2.104}
Find the equation of the plane which is at a distance of 7 units from the origin and normal to $\vec{n} = \myvec{3\\5\\-6}$
%
\section{Solution}
Given that,
\begin{itemize}
\item The normal vector to the plane is $\vec{n} = \myvec{3\\5\\-6}$.
\item The distance from origin d=7 units,
\begin{align}
    d&=\frac{\abs{c}}{\norm{\vec{n}}}
    \\
    c&=d\times{\norm{\vec{n}}}
    \\
    c&= 7\times \sqrt{3^2+5^2+(-6)^2}
    \\
    c&= 7\times \sqrt{70}
    \\
    c&=58.56
\end{align}
    \item  So,Equation of the plane is given by:-
\begin{align}
\vec{n}^T\vec{x} =c
\\
\myvec{3 & 5 & -6}{\vec{x}} = 58.56
\end{align}

\end{itemize}
